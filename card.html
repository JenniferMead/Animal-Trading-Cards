Narwhal

The Narwhal's famous tusk is actually its upper left tooth that is elongated! Mostly males have a tusk, but some females do as well and some males actually have two tusks!

  • Scientific Name: Monodon monoceros
  • Lifespan: Up to 50 years
  • Average Weight: 2 tons
  • Habitat: Arctic Seas

The Narwhal is famous for its single spiral tusk that sticks out of its head. Its closest living relative is the Beluga whale. Both lack dorsal fins so they can break through shallow ice in the cold Arctic seas if needed to catch their breath.
